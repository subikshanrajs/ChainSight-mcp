# ChainSight – 60-90 Second Demo Script
## For Hackathon Submission Video

---

### Pre-recording Checklist
- [ ] Streamlit UI running: `streamlit run ui/app.py`
- [ ] Browser at `http://localhost:8501`
- [ ] Test Mode toggle = **ON** (mock data, instant responses)
- [ ] Screen at 1920×1080, browser fullscreen
- [ ] Terminal hidden (not needed for demo)

---

## Script (60 seconds)

**[0:00 – 0:08]** — *Show landing page*

> "This is ChainSight — a reusable MCP skill that turns X Layer on-chain data
> into plain-English insights for AI agents and beginners."

*Point at the sidebar: wallet address, network, 3 tools listed.*

---

**[0:08 – 0:22]** — *Tab 1: Pool Health*

> "Let's check the health of the USDC/ETH pool."

1. Select `USDC/ETH` from dropdown
2. Click **Analyse Pool**
3. *Metrics appear: APY 7.2%, TVL $4.8M, LOW risk*

> "Instantly we see: 7.2% APY, $4.8 million in liquidity, and a LOW risk
> score — no jargon, just a clear answer."

---

**[0:22 – 0:38]** — *Tab 2: Swap Impact*

> "Now let's say I want to swap $50 USDC for ETH."

1. From: `USDC` | To: `ETH` | Amount: `50`
2. Click **Analyse Swap**
3. *Shows: ~0.0156 ETH, 0.12% slippage, Excellent quality*

> "ChainSight predicts my exact output, warns me about slippage,
> and confirms this is an excellent trade. All before I even open my wallet."

---

**[0:38 – 0:50]** — *Tab 3: Beginner Recommendation*

> "And for beginners who don't know where to start:"

1. Select **LOW – Safety first**
2. Click **Get My Recommendation**
3. *Shows: USDC/USDT, 2.9% APY, confidence 91%*

> "A tailored pool recommendation with a plain-English reason
> and exactly what to watch out for."

---

**[0:50 – 0:60]** — *Tab 4: Multi-Tool Chain*

> "The real power: chaining all three tools in one click."

1. Select `USDC/ETH`, amount `100`
2. Click **Run Full Chain**
3. *Progress bar fills, 3 summaries appear*

> "ChainSight checks pool health, predicts swap impact,
> and recommends the best pool — all in seconds.
> This is ChainSight — bringing pro-level on-chain intelligence to everyone."

## Recording Tips

- Use OBS or QuickTime for clean screen capture
- Keep mouse movements slow and deliberate
- Pause 1 second after each result appears before speaking
- Trim dead air at start/end
- Export at 1080p, 30fps, MP4
